Common questions

Everything you need to know

What exactly is Earnr AI?
Earnr AI is a personalized income discovery tool. It asks you a short set of smart questions about your skills, interests, and available time — then uses AI to generate realistic income ideas matched specifically to your profile, along with an Opportunity Score and a step-by-step Money Map.
Is this a get-rich-quick app?
Absolutely not — and we're proud to say that clearly. Earnr AI shows you realistic income paths based on real effort. Every idea includes an honest effort estimate and earning range. We don't make income guarantees because no one responsibly can.
How personalized are the results?
Deeply. Every income idea, Opportunity Score, and roadmap step is generated from your specific answers — not a template. Two people with different skills and schedules will get completely different plans.
Do you guarantee I'll make money?
No — and we won't pretend to. Earnr AI gives you a personalized plan built on realistic opportunities. Whether those opportunities turn into income depends on your effort, consistency, and execution.
Who is Earnr AI actually for?
Anyone who wants to make extra income but doesn't know where to start — or wants a clearer picture of which opportunity fits them best. Students, freelancers, creatives, parents with limited hours, and complete beginners.
What do I get with Premium vs. Free?
The free tier gives you one personalized income idea, your Opportunity Score, and a basic 3-step roadmap. Premium unlocks up to 5 ideas, an extended 8-10 step Money Map, deeper AI strategy, and advanced scoring.
How long does the onboarding take?
Less than 60 seconds for most people. We've deliberately kept the question flow short and focused. We ask only the questions that actually improve your results.
